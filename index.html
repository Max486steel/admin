How to Use This Portal

Use the search options above to look up a registrant's ID card status. Select your search type — ID Number, Form Number, or Name of Registrant — then enter the value and click Search.

Common Errors & What They Mean:

  • ⚠ No records found — The value entered does not match any record. Check the spelling or number and try again.
  • ⚠ Outside office hours — The portal is locked outside working hours. A supervisor password is required to continue.
  • ⚠ Too many failed attempts — Your account has been locked for 30 minutes due to repeated incorrect passwords.
  • ⚠ Session expired — You have been logged out automatically. Please sign in again.
  • ⚠ No internet connection — Check your network connection and try again. You will need to sign in again after reconnecting.
  • ⚠ Authentication error — Your login token is invalid. Refresh the page and sign in again.
